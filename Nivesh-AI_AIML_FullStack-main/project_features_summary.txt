PROJECT MISSION:
----------------
To democratize investing by removing fear and social barriers. Nivesh AI transforms 
complex financial markets into an easy-to-navigate journey, helping everyone 
learn, practice, and grow their wealth through advanced AI guidance.

CORE & EXCITING FEATURES:
-------------------------

1. 🤖 AGENTIC AI LANDING PAGE ASSISTANT (AI DOST)
   - Integrated with an autonomous Agentic AI system that doesn't just chat, 
     but analyzes your specific portfolio and market trends in real-time.
   - Capable of multi-step reasoning: from fetching stock metrics to 
     providing personalized behavioral insights.

2. 🎙️ GENERATIVE AI AUDIO AVATAR REPRESENTATION
   - Experience a lifelike 3D digital presence powered by Generative AI.
   - Features a high-fidelity audio avatar with synchronized lip-sync and 
     human-quality voice generation, making financial advice feel personal.
   - Uses cutting-edge TTS (Text-to-Speech) to transform complex data into 
     easy-to-understand spoken insights.

3. 🛡️ TRADEVERSE SANDBOX (RISK-FREE LEARNING)
   - A fully functional trading terminal where users can practice without 
     losing real money.
   - Build confidence using virtual capital while exploring Stocks, 
     Mutual Funds, and Cryptocurrency in a safe environment.

4. 🧠 BEHAVIORAL INTELLIGENCE ENGINE (NIVESHAI)
   - A unique AI engine that focuses on DECISIONS over profits.
   - Rewards discipline and risk management while flagging psychological 
     traps like "Panic Selling" or "Revenge Trading."
   - Specifically designed to build a healthy investor mindset from Day 1.

5. 👤 PERSONALIZED AI INVESTOR PROFILING
   - Intelligent onboarding that assesses your goals and risk tolerance.
   - Automatically recommends tailored strategies (Conservative to Aggressive) 
     and provides tips to help you transition from a beginner to a pro.

6. 🌍 MULTILINGUAL ACCESSIBILITY
   - Breaking the jargon barrier by providing AI responses in regional 
     languages.
   - Ensures that lack of English proficiency is never a barrier to 
     financial independence.

7. 🎮 GAMIFIED LEARNING & PROGRESSION
   - A structured journey with levels: Beginner, Intermediate, and Pro.
   - Track your "Credit Score" based on trading discipline and unlock 
     exclusive perks as your knowledge grows.

8. 📊 UNIFIED CROSS-ASSET DASHBOARD
   - A single, beautiful interface to track your entire wealth: 
     from Stocks and Mutual Funds to Cryptocurrencies.
   - No more switching between apps—everything you need is in one place.
